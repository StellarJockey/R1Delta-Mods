This mod adds new background art for the in-game lobby. Most of these were done by the amazing Tu Bui, the concept artist who worked on TF|1 and TF|2.

HOW TO APPLY:
There is currently no in-game way to swap out backgrounds, so you'll have to do it manually. I could only get this to work by hijacking a pre-existing .vtf file.

Go into: Aphelions.Custom_Backgrounds/materials/ui/menu
You'll see a bunch of folders called 'common', 'menu_imc', 'menu_demeter', etc... The 'common' folder controls what displays in game. To change it, simply copy and paste both .vtf files from your desired folder into 'common'. It's a bit inconvenient, but it works for now.

Good luck, have fun!
